Questions

3D printing quote basics.

What file types do you accept?

STL, 3MF, and OBJ files are ideal. I can also review sketches, reference photos, measurements, or links to existing models.

What materials can you print?

PLA, PETG, and TPU are the common options. ABS or ASA may be available by request depending on the part and use case.

How much does a print cost?

Price depends on model size, material, print time, quantity, and cleanup. Send the details and I’ll reply with a clear quote.

Can you help if I only have an idea?

Yes. Share the idea, rough dimensions, photos, or a sketch and I can help determine whether it is a good fit for 3D printing.

Do you offer pickup or shipping?

Local pickup is available in Rhode Island. Small shipped orders are available by request when pickup is not convenient.
